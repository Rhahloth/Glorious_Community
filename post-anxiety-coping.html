Published: April 2025 | Author: GCMHSC Clinical Team

Anxiety is a natural response to stress, but when it becomes overwhelming or persistent, it can interfere with daily life. At GCMHSC, we encounter many individuals who silently struggle with feelings of worry, fear, and unease.

What Causes Anxiety?

Anxiety can be triggered by various factors, including life transitions, past trauma, financial stress, work pressure, or even underlying health conditions. It's important to recognize that everyone experiences anxiety differently.

5 Simple Techniques to Manage Anxiety

  1. Deep Breathing: Take slow, deep breaths through your nose. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, and exhale for 4. Repeat.
  2. Limit Stimulants: Reduce caffeine and sugar intake, especially during stressful periods.
  3. Practice Mindfulness: Ground yourself in the present moment through guided meditation or quiet reflection.
  4. Physical Activity: Exercise releases endorphins that improve mood and lower anxiety levels.
  5. Talk to a Professional: Therapy can help unpack the root causes and build healthy coping strategies.
